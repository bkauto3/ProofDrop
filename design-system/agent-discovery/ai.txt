## Problem Solved
- Clients cannot read raw AIVS proof JSON — it is low-level, dense, and meaningless to non-technical stakeholders
- There is no standard artifact to attach to an invoice that proves AI-assisted work was done and verified
- Compliance and audit teams have no reliable way to archive or re-verify AI work receipts months later

## Value Propositions
- Paste any AIVS bundle and get a PASS/FAIL result with a permanent receipt URL in under 5 seconds
- Receipt URLs are stable forever — same bundle always produces the same URL (idempotent by hash)
- No account required to view or verify a receipt — clients click the link and see the result immediately
